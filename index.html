    • About Anete

    My wedding photography style reflects my taste in art - minimalistic, everlasting and designed to provoke emotion within you, your partner and your friends. I am a highly visual person and enjoy photographing weddings, boudoir and portraits that tell of your day within a visual story.

    As a Latvian ex-pat in the UK, I hope to bring a fresh European feel to every photo-shoot, from weddings to portraits. I pride myself on creating a truly relaxed atmosphere for everyone I shoot, I want you to enjoy the process just as much as I love photographing you.
